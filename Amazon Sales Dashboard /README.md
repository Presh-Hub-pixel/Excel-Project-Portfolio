## 📊 Project Overview

🛍️ **Understanding Amazon's Sales Performance**

The **Amazon Sales Performance Dashboard** is an Excel-based business intelligence solution designed to analyze revenue trends, product performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

Built using Microsoft Excel, this dashboard transforms transactional sales data into an interactive, executive-ready report that supports data-driven decision-making.

It enables stakeholders to:

* Monitor total revenue and order trends
* Identify top-performing products and customers
* Evaluate payment method preferences
* Detect operational bottlenecks in order fulfillment
* Understand seasonal and weekday purchasing behavior

---

## 📌 Business Use Case

This dashboard supports e-commerce decision-makers by answering key business questions:

* Which products generate the highest revenue?
* What are the monthly and seasonal sales trends?
* Which payment methods do customers prefer?
* How do cancellations and pending orders affect performance?
* What days drive the most sales activity?

The insights derived help improve marketing strategies, optimize operations, and drive revenue growth.

## 📈 Key Insights

💰🧊 **Refrigerators Dominate Revenue Contribution**
Refrigerators generated ₦78,000, significantly outperforming Laptops and Smartphones. High-ticket durable goods are the strongest revenue drivers despite lower order volume.

📦📉 **March Shows Strong Revenue, April Declines Sharply**
Revenue peaked at approximately ₦140,000 in March, followed by a sharp drop in April. This suggests seasonal patterns, stock limitations, or reduced marketing efforts.

💳💡 **Digital Payments Lead Customer Preference**
PayPal (60 transactions), Credit Card (54), and Debit Card (53) dominate usage, showing strong customer trust in digital payments.

🚚📊 **Order Fulfillment Requires Improvement**
Completed Orders: 35%
Pending Orders: 34%
Cancelled Orders: 31%
A total of 65% incomplete orders highlights operational inefficiencies.

📅📈 **Strong Weekday Sales, Weak Saturdays**
Monday (₦42,975) and Tuesday (₦42,650) lead sales, while Saturday (₦19,625) significantly underperforms.

📦🔌 **Electronics Drive Volume**
Electronics dominate with 348 units sold, followed by Clothing and Home Appliances. Books and Footwear show weak performance.

👩‍💼🏆 **High-Value Customers Drive Revenue**
Top customer Olivia Wilson contributed ₦36,170, emphasizing the importance of high-value customer retention.

## 🎯 Recommendations

🚀 **Intensify Marketing for High-Ticket Appliances**
Promote refrigerators and premium products through bundles, warranties, and installment payment options.

📅 **Stabilize Monthly Revenue Trends**
Launch targeted campaigns ahead of low-performing months to prevent sharp declines.

💳 **Strengthen Digital Payment Incentives**
Introduce cashback offers, loyalty rewards, and discounts for digital payments.

📦 **Improve Order Fulfillment Efficiency**
Reduce cancellations and pending orders through better inventory management, faster delivery, and real-time tracking systems.

📆 **Capitalize on Weekday Momentum**
Schedule major campaigns on Mondays and Tuesdays, and introduce weekend flash sales to boost Saturday performance.

📊 **Upsell Electronics**
Bundle electronics with accessories and warranties to increase average order value.

👑 **Develop Loyalty Programs**
Reward high-value customers with VIP perks to encourage repeat purchases.

📉 **Revitalize Underperforming Categories**
Use targeted promotions, bundles, and influencer marketing to improve performance in Books and Footwear.
